<p><p>Real Time Clocks (RTCs) are essential components in electronic devices, providing accurate timekeeping. The market for RTCs can be categorized into several types, including I2C RTCs, which utilize the Inter-Integrated Circuit protocol for communication, offering a simple two-wire interface. SPI RTCs, on the other hand, use the Serial Peripheral Interface, allowing faster data transfer rates. Other RTC types may include non-volatile memory-based RTCs and those featuring temperature compensation, catering to various application requirements in consumer, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
