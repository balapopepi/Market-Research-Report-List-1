<p><p>The foam caulks market comprises three main types: polyurethane, polystyrene, and phenolic. Polyurethane caulks are known for their strong adhesion and flexible sealing properties, making them suitable for various applications. Polystyrene caulks, on the other hand, offer excellent insulation performance and moisture resistance, often used in construction and packaging. Phenolic caulks provide superior thermal insulation and fire resistance, making them ideal for high-temperature environments. Each type serves distinct needs in construction, manufacturing, and maintenance industries.</p></p>
