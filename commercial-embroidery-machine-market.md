<p><p>The commercial embroidery machine market comprises two main types: single-head and multi-head embroidery machines. Single-head embroidery machines are designed for smaller operations, allowing for detailed work on individual items like jackets, hats, or shirts. Conversely, multi-head embroidery machines can simultaneously embroider multiple items, making them suitable for larger production runs and commercial operations that require efficiency and scalability. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to various business sizes and production demands within the embroidery sector.</p></p>
