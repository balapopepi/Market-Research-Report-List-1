<p><p>The roof solar mounting system market is categorized into flat roofs and pitched roofs. Flat roof systems are typically designed for commercial buildings, utilizing space for solar panels with ballasted or anchored mounts to optimize energy generation. In contrast, pitched roof systems suit residential properties, featuring structures that follow the roof's slope, often using racks for secure panel installation. Both types aim to maximize sunlight exposure while ensuring durability and ease of access for maintenance, catering to different architectural needs and preferences.</p></p>
