<p><p>The Variable Displacement Pump market consists of two primary types: Variable Displacement Piston Pumps and Variable Displacement Vane Pumps. Variable Displacement Piston Pumps utilize pistons to adjust the fluid displacement, providing higher efficiency and pressure control, making them ideal for applications requiring precise hydraulic power. In contrast, Variable Displacement Vane Pumps utilize sliding vanes that adjust to maintain flow rates and pressures. Both types offer flexibility in performance, making them suitable for diverse industrial, mobile, and automotive applications.</p></p>
