<p><p>Broker affiliate programs encompass various market types aimed at incentivizing partnerships. Partner programs involve brokers collaborating with affiliates to promote services, sharing profits from client referrals. Cost Per Action (CPA) programs compensate affiliates for specific actions taken by referred clients, such as signing up or making deposits. Introducer programs allow individuals or businesses to introduce potential clients to brokers, earning commissions based on the referred clients' activities. Each model leverages relationships to drive client acquisition and enhance revenue streams.</p></p>
