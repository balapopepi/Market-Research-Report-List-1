GPRS (General Packet Radio Service) enhances GSM by enabling packet-switching for more efficient data transfer. UMTS (Universal Mobile Telecommunications System) offers higher data rates and supports multimedia applications. Other emerging technologies like LTE and NB-IoT are expanding M2M capabilities, facilitating automation and IoT integration across diverse sectors.</p></p>
