<p><ul><li>Plane Profiling</li><li>Stereo Profiling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine tool profiling market encompasses various techniques for surface analysis, primarily divided into plane profiling and stereo profiling. Plane profiling involves measuring surface contours in a single plane, offering insights into surface roughness and texture. In contrast, stereo profiling utilizes three-dimensional data to provide a more comprehensive representation of surface characteristics, capturing depth and complexity. Both methods are essential in applications like quality control and material analysis, aiding manufacturers in enhancing product performance and ensuring precision in machining processes.</p></p>
