<p><p>The gonorrhea testing market encompasses various methods to detect the infection. Nucleic Acid Amplification (NAA) tests are highly sensitive and capable of identifying the bacteria's genetic material. Gram Stain identifies the presence of Neisseria gonorrhoeae through microscopy. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) detects antibodies against the infection. Gonorrhea Culture allows for the growth of bacteria from a sample, confirming the diagnosis. Rapid tests provide quick results, enhancing immediate treatment decisions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
