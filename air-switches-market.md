<p><strong>The Air Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bellows</li><li>Push Button</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air switches market includes two primary types: bellows and push buttons. Bellows air switches utilize a flexible diaphragm that activates the switch when air pressure changes, providing reliable operation in various applications. Push button air switches, on the other hand, feature a mechanical button that, when pressed, allows air to trigger the switch. Both types are used for controlling pneumatic systems, enhancing safety and efficiency in industries like manufacturing and automotive, where precise control is crucial.</p></p>
