<p><p>The steering angle sensors market comprises digital and analog sensors, each serving distinct applications in vehicle stability and control systems. Digital sensors provide precise, high-resolution data on the steering angle, enhancing accuracy in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). They utilize electronic components to output digital signals. Conversely, analog sensors generate continuous voltage signals corresponding to the steering angle, making them simpler and more cost-effective yet less precise than their digital counterparts. Each type plays a crucial role in modern automotive technology.</p></p>
