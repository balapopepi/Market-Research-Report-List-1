<p><ul><li>Physical Processes</li><li>Chemical Processes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanocomposite film market can be categorized into two main types based on production processes: physical and chemical. Physical processes involve methods like extrusion or melt blending to integrate nanoparticles within a polymer matrix, emphasizing ease and rapid production. In contrast, chemical processes, such as sol-gel techniques or chemical vapor deposition, focus on the chemical interactions and transformations to create more uniform and enhanced properties at the nanoscale. Each method offers distinct advantages in terms of performance and application suitability.</p></p>
