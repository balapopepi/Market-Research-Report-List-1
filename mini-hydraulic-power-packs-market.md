<p><p>Mini hydraulic power packs are categorized into two primary types based on their power sources: DC power units and AC power units. DC power units operate on direct current, making them ideal for mobile applications and environments where electrical outlets are scarce. They offer portability and efficiency for smaller, demanding tasks. Conversely, AC power units use alternating current and are typically used in stationary applications, providing higher power output for larger hydraulic systems in industrial settings. Both types cater to diverse hydraulic needs.</p></p>
