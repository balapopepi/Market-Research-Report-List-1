<p><p>The sputter targets market is categorized into three main types: metal targets, alloy targets, and ceramic compound targets. Metal targets, made from pure metals like gold or tungsten, are used in various deposition processes due to their excellent conductivity. Alloy targets consist of two or more metals, providing tailored properties for specific applications. Ceramic compound targets, composed of non-metallic materials, offer unique characteristics such as high resistance to wear and corrosion, making them suitable for advanced electronic and optical coatings.</p></p>
