<p><p>The Excimer Laser Gas Market consists of two primary types: Balance Gas Ne (Neon) and Balance Gas He (Helium). Balance Gas Ne involves the use of Neon to support the laser's operational efficiency, often producing shorter wavelengths optimal for various applications, including medical and industrial sectors. Conversely, Balance Gas He utilizes Helium, enhancing the cooling and stability of laser performance. Both types play crucial roles in improving laser output, enabling advanced technologies in laser dentistry, dermatology, and materials processing.</p></p>
