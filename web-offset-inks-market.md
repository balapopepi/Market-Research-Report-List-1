<p><p>The Web Offset Inks market is primarily divided into two types: coldset inks and heatset inks. Coldset inks are used in uncoated paper applications, allowing rapid drying without heat, making them ideal for newspaper printing. In contrast, heatset inks are applied to coated papers and require drying through heat, resulting in more vibrant colors and a glossy finish, suitable for magazines and commercial printing. Each type caters to specific printing needs and enhances print quality based on the application.</p></p>
