<p><ul><li>Distributed Fiber Optic Temperature Sensor</li><li>Fiber Optic Fluorescence Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic temperature sensor market encompasses various types, primarily distributed fiber optic temperature sensors and fiber optic fluorescence temperature sensors. Distributed fiber optic temperature sensors utilize the entire length of a fiber to provide continuous temperature monitoring over vast areas, enhancing precision in applications like pipelines and power plants. In contrast, fiber optic fluorescence temperature sensors employ luminescent materials that change their emission characteristics with temperature variations, enabling localized and high-resolution temperature measurement, suitable for intricate research and industrial settings.</p></p>
