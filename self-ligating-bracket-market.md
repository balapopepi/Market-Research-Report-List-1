<p><ul><li>Passive Type</li><li>Active Type</li><li>Interactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-ligating bracket market is categorized into three types: passive, active, and interactive. Passive self-ligating brackets allow the archwire to slide freely without friction, promoting comfortable movement while minimizing treatment time. Active self-ligating brackets exert constant force on the archwire, enhancing tooth movement. Interactive self-ligating brackets combine features of both passive and active types, allowing orthodontists to adjust treatment based on specific patient needs. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various patient and treatment requirements.</p></p>
