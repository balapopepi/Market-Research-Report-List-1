<p><p>Piston samplers are categorized into three main market types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual samplers rely on human operation, providing a cost-effective solution for lightweight applications. Electric samplers utilize motors to automate the sampling process, ensuring consistent and efficient collection, ideal for medium to heavy-duty tasks. Hydraulic samplers, powered by fluid pressure, excel in demanding environments, offering high force and precision for extracting samples from deep or challenging locations. Each type serves unique operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
