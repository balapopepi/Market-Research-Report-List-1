<p><p>Anti Snake Venom Serum (ASVS) is crucial for treating snakebite envenomations. The market is categorized into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent serums. Polyvalent ASVS is designed to treat bites from multiple snake species, offering broad-spectrum efficacy and convenience in areas with diverse snake populations. In contrast, monovalent ASVS targets specific snake species, providing tailored treatment for particular envenomations, often resulting in higher efficacy for those specific bites, albeit with limited applicability across different snake types.</p></p>
