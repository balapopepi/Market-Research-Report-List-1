<p><p>The travertine market is primarily categorized into two types: Magnesian Travertine and High-Calcium Travertine. Magnesian Travertine contains significant magnesium carbonate, contributing to its unique coloration and enhanced durability, making it popular for aesthetic and structural applications. High-Calcium Travertine, rich in calcium carbonate, is valued for its natural beauty and versatility in construction and decor. Both types cater to distinct market needs, with various applications in architecture, landscaping, and interior design, influencing pricing and demand dynamics.</p></p>
