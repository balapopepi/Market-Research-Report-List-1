<p><strong>The Hidden Camera Detection Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF Based</li><li>LED Light Gauge Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hidden camera detection devices mainly include RF-based and LED light gauge-based technologies. RF-based detectors identify hidden cameras by sensing the radio frequency signals emitted by wireless devices, effectively locating cameras that may be transmitting data. On the other hand, LED light gauge-based detectors utilize specific light wavelengths to reveal lens reflections from cameras, making hidden devices visible in various environments. Both methods cater to increasing privacy concerns, enhancing security in personal and professional settings.</p></p>
