<p><strong>The Surgical Drainage Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Drains</li><li>Active Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drainage tube market is segmented into two main types: passive drains and active drains. Passive drains rely on gravity to remove fluids from surgical sites, allowing drainage to occur naturally without additional intervention. Examples include Penrose drains and suction drains. In contrast, active drains utilize suction mechanisms, such as vacuum systems, to actively remove fluids, ensuring more efficient drainage. These devices are essential in preventing fluid accumulation and reducing the risk of infection post-surgery.</p></p>
