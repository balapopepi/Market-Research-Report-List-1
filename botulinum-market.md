<p><p>The botulinum market consists primarily of two types: antitoxins and antibiotics. Antitoxins are immunological treatments that neutralize botulinum toxins, offering effective management for botulism cases. They are crucial in preventing severe complications associated with the disease. On the other hand, antibiotics target the bacteria producing the toxins, facilitating the elimination of the pathogen from the body. Together, these market segments play vital roles in addressing botulism, contributing to public health initiatives aimed at reducing the incidence of this rare but serious illness.</p></p>
