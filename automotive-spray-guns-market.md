<p><ul><li>High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP)</li><li>Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive spray guns market is primarily categorized into High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP), Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP), and other types. HVLP guns operate with a high air volume at low pressure, providing efficient paint application and reduced overspray, making them ideal for large surfaces. LVLP guns use less air volume, requiring less pressure while delivering fine atomization, suitable for detailed work. Other types include conventional spray guns and airless systems, each tailored for specific painting requirements and outcomes.</p></p>
