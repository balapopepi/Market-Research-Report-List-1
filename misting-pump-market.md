<p><p>The misting pump market is categorized into three primary types: low pressure, mid pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure misting pumps operate at lower water pressure, suitable for residential and small outdoor areas, offering a gentle cooling effect. Mid pressure pumps provide increased misting efficiency, ideal for commercial applications like patios and restaurants. High pressure misting pumps operate at significantly higher pressure, creating ultra-fine mist droplets, which are effective in industrial settings and large outdoor spaces for maximum cooling and humidity control.</p></p>
