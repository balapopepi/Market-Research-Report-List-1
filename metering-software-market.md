<p><p>Metering software is categorized into two main types: online software and offline software. Online software operates through the cloud, providing real-time access and updates, making it ideal for remote monitoring and management of utilities. It often supports collaborative features and data analytics. This type is often favored for environments with limited internet access or stringent data security requirements.</p></p>
