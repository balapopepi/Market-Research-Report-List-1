<p><strong>The Human Injection Flu Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trivalent Influenza Vaccine</li><li>Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Injection Flu Vaccine market comprises two main types: trivalent and quadrivalent influenza vaccines. Trivalent vaccines are designed to protect against three flu virus strains, typically two A strains and one B strain. In contrast, quadrivalent vaccines offer broader protection by targeting four strains—two A strains and two B strains. The quadrivalent option is becoming increasingly popular due to its enhanced efficacy and ability to cover more circulating flu viruses, appealing to a wider demographic for vaccination.</p></p>
