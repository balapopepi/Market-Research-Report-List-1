<p><p>The aerospace grade acrylic market encompasses two primary types: acrylic cast plates and acrylic extrusion sheets. Acrylic cast plates are produced through a casting process, resulting in high optical clarity and thickness options, making them ideal for aircraft windows and displays. In contrast, acrylic extrusion sheets are manufactured via an extrusion process, offering more uniform thickness and cost-effectiveness, suitable for various applications like flight instrument panels and protective covers. Both types provide lightweight, durable alternatives to glass, enhancing design flexibility in aerospace applications.</p></p>
