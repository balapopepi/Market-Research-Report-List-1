<p><p>The TiAl (Titanium Aluminide) alloy market is segmented into Gamma Type and Other Type. Other Type alloys include α2-Ti3Al and B2-TiAl variants, offering unique properties like enhanced creep resistance and ductility, catering to specialized applications in high-temperature environments. Both types are critical for lightweight, high-performance components in advanced engineering sectors.</p></p>
