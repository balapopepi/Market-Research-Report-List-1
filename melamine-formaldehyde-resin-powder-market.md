<p><p>The melamine formaldehyde resin powder market is primarily segmented into two types: methylation melamine formaldehyde resin and unmethylated melamine formaldehyde resin. Methylation melamine formaldehyde resin is chemically modified to enhance properties like water resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for coatings, adhesives, and laminates. In contrast, unmethylated melamine formaldehyde resin maintains a more basic structure, offering high bonding strength and rigidity, often utilized in the production of molded products and high-pressure laminates. Both types serve diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
