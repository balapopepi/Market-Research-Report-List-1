<p><p>The night vision goggle market primarily comprises two types: LLL (Light Leveling and Long-range) night vision goggles and infrared night vision goggles. LLL goggles enhance available light for clear visibility in low-light conditions, suitable for tactical and recreational use. In contrast, infrared night vision goggles utilize thermal imaging technology to detect heat signatures, enabling visibility in complete darkness. Both types cater to different needs, including military, law enforcement, and outdoor activities, highlighting their essential roles in enhancing nighttime visibility.</p></p>
