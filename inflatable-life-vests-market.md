<p><p>The inflatable life vests market is primarily categorized into manual and auto inflatable life vests. Manual inflatable life vests require the wearer to pull a cord to activate the inflation mechanism, offering a straightforward and cost-effective option. In contrast, auto inflatable life vests automatically inflate upon submersion in water, ensuring immediate flotation without the need for conscious action. Both types are designed for safety in aquatic environments, catering to different user preferences and scenarios.</p></p>
