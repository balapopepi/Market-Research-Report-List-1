<p><p>The grouting service market encompasses various types used in construction and ground stabilization. Compaction grouting involves injecting a controlled volume of grout to increase soil density, enhancing stability. Polyurethane grouting uses polymer-based materials to fill voids and waterproof structures, making it efficient for repairs. Cellular grouting, utilizing lightweight, air-infused grout, is often employed for buoyancy reduction and filling large voids. Other methods include cementitious and acrylate grouting, which cater to specific engineering needs and challenges.</p></p>
