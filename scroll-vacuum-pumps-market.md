<p><strong>The Scroll Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided moving scroll</li><li>Double-sided moving scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scroll vacuum pumps market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided moving scroll types. Single-sided moving scroll pumps utilize a fixed scroll and a moving scroll to create vacuum, making them compact and efficient for applications requiring low to medium vacuum levels. In contrast, double-sided moving scroll pumps feature two moving scrolls, enhancing pumping efficiency and achieving deeper vacuums, suitable for more demanding industrial processes. Both types are valued for their low noise, minimal vibration, and reliable performance in various sectors.</p></p>
