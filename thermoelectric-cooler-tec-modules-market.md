<p><strong>The Thermoelectric Cooler (TEC) Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multistage Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoelectric Cooler (TEC) modules are categorized into single-stage and multistage types based on their design and cooling capacity. Single-stage modules consist of one thermoelectric element, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate cooling efficiency and compact size. In contrast, multistage modules contain multiple thermoelectric elements stacked together, providing enhanced cooling capabilities for demanding applications like laser cooling or medical devices. The choice between single-stage and multistage TECs depends on specific cooling requirements, thermal load, and space constraints.</p></p>
