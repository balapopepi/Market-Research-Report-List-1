<p><p>Fluoroplastic tubes are specialized polymer tubes known for their exceptional chemical resistance, thermal stability, and electrical insulation. **PTFE tubes** (Polytetrafluoroethylene) offer high-temperature resistance and low friction. **PFA tubes** (Perfluoroalkoxy) combine PTFE's properties with improved flexibility and transparency. **FEP tubes** (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) provide similar benefits with enhanced clarity and lower melting points. **ETFE tubes** (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) are lightweight, durable, and resistant to UV and abrasion. These tubes are widely used in industries like chemical processing, medical, and automotive for their reliability and performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
