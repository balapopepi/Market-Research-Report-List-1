<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage indicators market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact voltage indicators require physical contact with the electrical circuit to detect voltage levels, ensuring accuracy but potentially exposing the user to safety risks. In contrast, non-contact voltage indicators allow users to detect voltage from a distance, enhancing safety by minimizing direct interaction with live circuits. Both types serve essential roles in electrical maintenance, with their applications influenced by user safety preferences and specific operational needs.</p></p>
