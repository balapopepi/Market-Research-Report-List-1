<p><p>The Dry Topping Dispenser market is segmented into two primary types: Wall-Mount and Countertop dispensers. Wall-Mount dispensers are designed for vertical installation, saving counter space while providing easy access to toppings. They are ideal for establishments with limited counter area. In contrast, Countertop dispensers are positioned directly on surfaces, making them easily portable and accessible for customers. These units often serve high-volume settings, offering convenience and visibility, enhancing the overall dining experience. Both types cater to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
