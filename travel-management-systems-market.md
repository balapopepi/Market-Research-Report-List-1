<p><p>Travel Management Systems (TMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems require organizations to install software directly on their servers, providing more control over data and customization but needing significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based TMS solutions operate online, offering greater flexibility, easy accessibility, and lower upfront costs. This model facilitates automatic updates and scalability, making it suitable for businesses seeking efficiency in managing travel-related processes.</p></p>
