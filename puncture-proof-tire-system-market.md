<p><p>The puncture-proof tire system market includes two primary types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric that crisscross each other, offering durability and strength, making them well-suited for heavy loads and rough terrain. In contrast, radial tires consist of plies arranged radially from the center, providing better fuel efficiency, improved handling, and a smoother ride. Both types are increasingly integrated with puncture-proof technology to enhance safety and extend tire life in various applications.</p></p>
