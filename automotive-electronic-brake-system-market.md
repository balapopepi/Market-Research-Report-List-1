<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Brake System market encompasses various technologies aimed at enhancing vehicle safety and performance. Traction Control System (TCS) prevents wheel spin during acceleration, optimizing grip on slippery surfaces. Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) avoids wheel lockup during hard braking, maintaining steering control. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) detects and reduces loss of traction, helping drivers maintain control in skids. Together, these systems significantly improve vehicle stability, safety, and overall driving experience, driving their adoption in modern vehicles.</p></p>
