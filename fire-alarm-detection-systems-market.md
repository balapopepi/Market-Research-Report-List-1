<p><strong>The Fire Alarm Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Dual Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire alarm detection systems are categorized mainly into three types: photoelectric, ionization, and dual sensor. Photoelectric alarms use light-detection technology to sense smoke, making them effective for slow-burning fires. Ionization alarms detect fast-flaming fires through ionized air currents caused by smoke particles. Dual sensor alarms combine both technologies, offering a comprehensive solution by detecting a broader range of fire types. This variety ensures enhanced safety and timely alerts, crucial for effective fire risk management in various environments.</p></p>
