<p><p>Shell and tube heat exchangers are pivotal in transferring heat between two fluids, typically used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation. Their design consists of a series of tubes enclosed within a shell; one fluid flows through the tubes while the other flows around them in the shell, facilitating efficient heat transfer. This technology is favored for its robustness, ease of maintenance, and ability to handle high pressure and temperature conditions.</p><p>The Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers Market is poised for substantial growth, driven by increasing industrial activities, heightened energy efficiency requirements, and evolving environmental regulations.
