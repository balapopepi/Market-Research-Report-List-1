<p><p>Whole house water filtration systems are categorized primarily into single-stage, dual-stage, and triple-stage types based on the number of filtration processes used. Single-stage systems filter water through one cartridge, efficiently removing basic impurities. Dual-stage systems add an extra filtration layer, targeting specific contaminants for improved quality. Triple-stage systems employ three distinct filtration processes, ensuring comprehensive purification by addressing a wider range of pollutants and enhancing water taste and safety. Each type serves varying household needs and contaminant concerns.</p></p>
