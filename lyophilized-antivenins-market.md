<p><p>The lyophilized antivenins market is categorized into three main types: neurotoxin, blood toxin, and mixed toxin. Neurotoxin antivenins are designed to counteract venom that affects the nervous system, often from snake bites. Blood toxin antivenins target venoms that impair blood coagulation or promote hemorrhage. Mixed toxin antivenins provide a broader treatment spectrum, addressing venoms that may contain both neurotoxic and blood-toxic components. Each type plays a crucial role in treating specific types of envenomation.</p></p>
