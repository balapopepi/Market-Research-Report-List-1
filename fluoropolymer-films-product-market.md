PTFE films are known for their exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance applications. PVDF films offer good mechanical strength and are often used in electrical insulation. FEP films combine transparency with chemical resistance, suitable for packaging and other applications. The "Others" category includes specialty fluoropolymer films that meet niche market demands.</p></p>
