<p><p>The silicone rubber adhesive market features several key types, each tailored for specific applications. High-Temperature Vulcanized (HTV) silicone rubber is durable and ideal for high-heat environments. Room Temperature Vulcanized (RTV) silicone rubber cures at ambient temperatures, suitable for various bonding and sealing applications. Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is processed in liquid form, enabling intricate molds and high precision in production. Each type offers unique properties, catering to industries like automotive, electronics, and healthcare for enhanced performance and reliability.</p></p>
