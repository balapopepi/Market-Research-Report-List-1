<p><p>The solvent polyurethane adhesive market is segmented into three main types: polyether, polyester, and mixed types. Polyether adhesives are known for their excellent elasticity and moisture resistance, making them suitable for flexible applications. Polyester adhesives offer superior strength and durability, often used in demanding environments. Mixed type adhesives combine the properties of both polyether and polyester, providing versatility for a wide range of applications. Each type serves distinct industry needs, from automotive to construction, ensuring optimal bonding performance.</p></p>
