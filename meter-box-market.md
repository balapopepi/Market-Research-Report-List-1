<p><p>The meter box market primarily includes single-phase and polyphase meter boxes. Single-phase meter boxes are designed for residential applications, managing energy consumption for homes or small businesses, typically with a lower voltage and current capacity. In contrast, polyphase meter boxes cater to commercial and industrial settings, supporting multiple phases of electricity supply, allowing for higher loads and more complex energy management needs. Both types are essential for accurate metering and efficient energy distribution in varying applications.</p></p>
