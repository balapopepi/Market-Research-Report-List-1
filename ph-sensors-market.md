<p><p>The pH sensors market comprises various types, including glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors are traditional and utilize a glass membrane to measure hydrogen ion concentration, providing accurate readings in various solutions. ISFET sensors, or Ion-Sensitive Field Effect Transistor sensors, offer advantages like miniaturization and resistance to fouling, making them suitable for harsh environments. Other sensors may include solid-state, optical, or capacitive pH sensors, each offering unique features and applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
