<p><p>The corrosion monitoring solution market is dominated by two main types: non-intrusive and intrusive technologies. Non-intrusive technologies utilize methods like ultrasonic testing and electrochemical techniques, allowing for real-time assessment without disrupting operations or damaging materials. In contrast, intrusive technologies involve direct physical access to structures, employing techniques such as corrosion coupons and probes, which require equipment disassembly or shutdown for accurate measurement. Both types serve to enhance asset integrity and reduce maintenance costs in various industries, including oil and gas, utilities, and manufacturing.</p></p>
