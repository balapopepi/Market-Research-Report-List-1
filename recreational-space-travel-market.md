<p><p>The recreational space travel market encompasses two primary types: suborbital and orbital. Suborbital travel involves brief journeys that reach the edge of space, allowing participants to experience weightlessness and stunning views of Earth without completing an orbit. In contrast, orbital travel involves missions that achieve sustained orbit around Earth, offering extended experiences in microgravity. Each market caters to different expectations and budgets, with suborbital being more accessible and shorter, while orbital travel provides a more immersive and prolonged adventure in space.</p></p>
