<p><p>CTC Batteries encompasses two primary market types: Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries and Ternary Lithium-ion batteries. Lithium Iron Phosphate batteries are known for their safety, thermal stability, and long cycle life, making them ideal for applications like electric vehicles and renewable energy storage. In contrast, Ternary Lithium-ion batteries, which contain lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide, offer higher energy density and efficiency, catering to high-performance applications such as smartphones and electric vehicles, where lightweight and compact design is essential.</p></p>
