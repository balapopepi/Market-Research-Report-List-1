<p><p>The 3D packaging market encompasses three main types: 3D Wire Bonding, 3D TSV (Through-Silicon Via), and others. </p><p>3D Wire Bonding involves connecting integrated circuits using fine wires, facilitating efficient space utilization. 3D TSV incorporates vertical interconnections through silicon wafers for improved performance and reduced signal latency. Other methods include advanced techniques like chip-on-chip and fan-out packaging, which enhance functionality and thermal management. Together, these technologies drive innovation in electronics, enabling compact designs and higher performance in semiconductor applications.</p></p>
