<p><p>The TWS (True Wireless Stereo) in-ear monitor market is categorized into two main types based on noise cancellation technology: Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and Passive Noise Canceling. ANC employs built-in microphones to detect external sounds and generate counteracting sound waves, effectively reducing background noise. In contrast, Passive Noise Canceling relies on the physical design and materials of the earphones to block external sounds, providing a snug fit that minimizes noise intrusion. Both types enhance audio experiences in different ways.</p></p>
