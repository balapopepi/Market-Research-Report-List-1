Low refractive index lenses typically offer basic optical performance and are cost-effective, suitable for simple eyewear. Medium refractive index lenses balance quality and affordability, providing enhanced thickness reduction while maintaining clarity. High refractive index lenses are designed for advanced optical applications, offering superior thinness and lightweight features, ideal for high prescriptions and specialized uses. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
