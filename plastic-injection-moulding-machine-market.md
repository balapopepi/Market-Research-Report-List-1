<p><p>The plastic injection molding machine market is categorized primarily into PET and PVC injection molding machines. PET injection molding machines are designed for producing items like bottles and containers, using polyethylene terephthalate, which offers superior clarity and strength. PVC injection molding machines, on the other hand, specialize in processing polyvinyl chloride, commonly used for pipes, fittings, and flexible products due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness. Both types are essential for various applications across industries, including packaging, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
