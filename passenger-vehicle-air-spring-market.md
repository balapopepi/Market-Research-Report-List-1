<p><p>The passenger vehicle air spring market features three primary types: convoluted, sleeve, and rolling lobe. Convoluted air springs consist of a series of tapered layers, providing flexibility and load-bearing capability, ideal for cushioning. Sleeve air springs are characterized by their cylindrical shape, offering a simple design with ample stroke length and support for ride comfort. Rolling lobe air springs, featuring a bellows design, enable smooth motion and enhanced stability, making them suitable for performance-driven vehicles. Each type caters to specific ride quality and performance needs.</p></p>
