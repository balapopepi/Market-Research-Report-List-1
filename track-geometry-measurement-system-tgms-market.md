<p><strong>The Track Geometry Measurement System (TGMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Contact Based Track Geometry Measurement System(TGMS)</li><li>Contact Based Track Geometry Measurement System(TGMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Track Geometry Measurement System (TGMS) is crucial for assessing the condition and alignment of railway tracks. It is categorized into two main types: No Contact Based TGMS and Contact Based TGMS. </p><p>No Contact Based TGMS utilizes advanced sensors and technologies, such as lasers and cameras, to measure track geometry without physical contact, ensuring minimal disruption to operations. In contrast, Contact Based TGMS involves equipment that physically interacts with the tracks, providing precise measurements through mechanical means, yet may affect train schedules during assessments.</p></p>
