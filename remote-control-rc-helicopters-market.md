<p><p>Remote Control (RC) helicopters are categorized based on their channels, which determine their control complexity and capabilities. Three-channel helicopters allow basic maneuvering, enabling up, down, and turning, making them suitable for beginners. Four-channel helicopters add additional control for forward and backward flight, offering more versatility. Six-channel helicopters provide advanced control options, including aerobatics and precise movements, catering to experienced pilots. Each type serves a different skill level, enhancing user experience and engagement in the RC hobby.</p></p>
