Zeolite catalysts feature porous structures that facilitate shape-selective catalysis, ideal for applications in refining and petrochemicals. Other catalysts include organometallic compounds and enzymes, catering to diverse chemical processes across multiple industries, enhancing reaction rates and product yields.</p></p>
